Banners and Events

How to get EX, LD, and BT Weapons from Banners

If you go to Draws/Shops you can see a series of banners with different characters. This is how you get you get EX, LD, and BT Weapons. If you have draw tickets you can do a single draw. In single draws you have a 0.75 chance for a EX, a 0.5 chance to get and LD, and a 0.1 chance for a BT. If you don't have any draw tickets you can spend 500 gems to do a single draw. A multi-draw is 5,000 gems, it's 10 single draws with another draw that's guranteed to be a 5 Star. The rates for the +1 draw for multi-draws are 7.5 for EX, 5 for an LD, and a 3 for a BT.

G Tokens

To obtain G Tokens is to use multi-draws, you get 20 G Tokens per multi-draw. G tokens can be spent on the prizes in the G Token exchange for the specific banner. If you have G Tokens on one banner, it can only be used on that banner, nothing else. The prizes the G Token exchange have are Power Stones for 100 G Tokens each. A Book of Ruin's Mysteries is for 200 G Tokens each, a EX and LD weapon are 300 G Tokens each. And a BT is worth 500 G Tokens each.

A Character's Event

A event is where a new character is showcased through a series of battles and cutscenes. Unlike a lost chapter, it is just battles and cutscenes.

Heretics

Heretics is a certain event where there are series of battles. If you use a party for one battle, you can't use that party again unless you do the battle again with another party.

Raids

A raid is where different players team up to beat a certain boss. The way a raid works is that one player fights the boss and when the player beats it the main boss takes damage. The goal is to get the main boss's HP to 0.
